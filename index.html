Monitor your fridge

Know at what temperature your fridge is running, and never have to worry again that it turned off in the middle of the night, secretly spoiling your food. You can also rest assured that food in the front of the fridge is kept as fresh as food in the back.

Most Fridgigator features

Logging

Log fridge conditions and see how it’s performing over time.

Network connectivity

Uses a mesh network to connect to the server, ensuring that even if one sensor stops working, the rest of the data will find its way through.

Open Source

All the code is open source, and most of it can be built with open source tooling.

Alerts

You’ll know if your fridge stops working even when on vacation or in the middle of the night through your cell-phone.

Optimizing food placement

Keep your produce in the best part of the fridge. Vegetables need to be kept under different humidity and temperature settings than fruits, and you’ll now know which part of your fridge is the optimal place for your veggies.

Save energy

Do you need to keep your Fridge on such a low temperature? By looking at the data, you can adjust your Fridge’s temperature settings, saving money and the environment.
